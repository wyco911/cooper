Cooper Union Student Protests


General Description:
There are two stakeholder communities that were impacted by the construction
of 41 Cooper Square the neighbourhood of The East Village / Bowery and
Cooper Union as an institution. This research is interested in understanding
the reactions of these two communities to the project - initial pushback and
concerns over the changing character of the neighbourhood, increased budgetary
issues for Cooper Union, the relationship of the building with the St. George
Ukrainian Catholic Church, and the reaction of Cooperâ€™s students, faculty and
staff to name a few. This report will be a survey of the inclusion or
exclusion of these parties to committees that were involved with the
construction/development of the New Academic Building. We will try to answer
which communities was the NAB targeted towards as a branding tool and which
communities were excluded from the decision process.
