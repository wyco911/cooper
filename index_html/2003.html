February, Promises are broken


General Description:

In 2003, contrary to reassurances by Cooper’s board and the recommendations of the Joint Task force, a 22 storey building is proposed on the site of a parking lot, 26 Astor place, owned by Cooper Union. This wasn’t a part of the original development plan, but the special permit was issued as an extension of original 2001 proposal use regulations.

References:

1. “...it was not included, yet only a few months after the approval of the Large Scale Plan this application for a 22-story building is introduced; and Cooper Union's representatives stated only months ago that 26 Astor Place would be built as-of right - and this application is not for as-of-right uses, and we are dismayed by the breaking of this promise...” Community board 2 minutes, February 20, 2003, p.12, http://www.nyc.gov/html/mancb2/downloads/pdf/fullboard_2003/02february2003.pdf
