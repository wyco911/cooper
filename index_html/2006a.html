Crisis


General Description:

When the stock market collapsed in late 2008, the damage already promised by the plan's failure was greatly magnified, and transformed the entire project into an albatross. A large amount of the loan secured to fund construction was put into Hedge Funds before the market collapsed and was lost. President Campbell gave an interview to the New York Times in February 2008 about Cooper Union finances
