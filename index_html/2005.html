The First Loan


General Description:

In 2006, Cooper Union borrows $175 million as part of a complex and risky plan promoted by then-President George Campbell to finance a new academic building. Sufficient funds aren’t raised to complete the project even after the large loan. Cooper’s only meaningful asset - the land under the Chrysler Building is Mortgaged against the loan. The groundbreaking and construction begins.
