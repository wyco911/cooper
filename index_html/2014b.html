Ivory Tower Documentary


General Description:

The Documentary captures a large part of the student protest with regard to Free Tuition. The film itself follows a thesis that Higher Education Institutions in America today are increasingly concerned with the image they portray to fellow students and peer institutions, and that the focus is on treating a college education as a business model for most for-profit colleges and universities.
